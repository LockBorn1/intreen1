Open **My Courses**, click the **Resume** button on the course card, or use **Bookmarks**.
Where can I find my certificates?
Certificates live under **Certificates**. You can also access them from your **Transcript** once issued.
Can I see upcoming deadlines?
Check the **Calendar** card and **Announcements** for scheduled events and deadlines.
